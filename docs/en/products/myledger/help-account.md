## How to add, delete, and modify an account?
Slide your finger left on a account, which reveals the actions you can do on that account. See the figure below.
You can google the [swipe gesture](https://www.google.com/search?q=ios+gesture+swipe+list+item) if you are not familiar with this operation.

![](/assets/images/MyLedger/account/swipe-actions.png)

## How to jump to the other account?
1. Slide your finger left on a transaction, which reveals the actions you can do on that transaction. See the figure below.
2. Tap the 'Jump' button.

![](/assets/images/MyLedger/account/jump-to-the-other-account.png)
