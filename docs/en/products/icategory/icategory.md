# iCategory: Home Inventory List
    
[![Download on the App Store](https://toolbox.marketingtools.apple.com/api/v2/badges/download-on-the-app-store/black/en-us?releaseDate=1754870400 "Download on the App Store")](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/gnucash-helper-manage-money/id6738621485?itscg=30200&itsct=apps_box_badge&mttnsubad=6738621485)

iCategory is a category-based personal stuff management app. It enables tracking, organizing, and managing inventory, materials, collectables, tools, and any items. Whether managing a home stuff or small business assets, iCategory turns chaos into order at home, at work, and anywhere else.

Key Features:
1. Dynamic Categorization: Used to classify your items. Move beyond rigid folders. Our flexible system adapts to any item type for ultimate retrieval speed.
2. Rich Media Documentation: Attach photos, videos, and essential documents to every item, keeping all your data in one place.
3. Flexible Inventory Lists: Generate tailored inventory lists based on your unique categories. And export them into editable formats for seamless reporting.
